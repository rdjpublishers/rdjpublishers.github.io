Free Online Tools & Apps by RDJ Publishers

RDJ Publishers is a growing collection of free web-based tools, browser games, and productivity utilities designed for students and everyday users. Every tool works instantly in your browser — no download, no login, and no charge. Ever.

What Can You Find Here?

Browse tools across five categories: Tools for everyday calculations, Games you can play instantly, Utilities that make daily tasks easier, Fun interactive experiences, and Productivity apps to keep you organised. New projects are added regularly.

Why RDJ Publishers?

All tools are built with simplicity in mind — they load fast, work on mobile, and require no technical knowledge. Whether you're a student tracking attendance, a casual gamer, or someone who needs a quick utility, RDJ Publishers has something for you.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025–2026

RDJpublishers ("we", "us", or "our") operates the RDJ Publishers website. This page explains our policies on data collection, use, and disclosure.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect any personal information. All data such as project edits and preferences are stored only on your own device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

Cookies

This website does not set tracking or advertising cookies directly. Preferences such as admin session state are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Third-Party Services

This site uses Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect usage data as per their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This service does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
